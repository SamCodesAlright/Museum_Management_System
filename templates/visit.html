Accessibility

Mobility Access

All public areas are wheelchair accessible with elevators connecting all floors. Wheelchairs available for loan.

Hearing Assistance

Audio guides with induction loops available. ASL interpreters can be scheduled with two weeks notice.

Visual Assistance

Tactile gallery guides and large print materials available at information desk.

Visitor Policies

  • Non-flash photography permitted in permanent collections
  • Large bags (>30L) must be checked at coat room
  • Food and drink restricted to café areas only
  • Sketching permitted with pencil only (no ink)
  • Cell phone conversations in designated areas only
