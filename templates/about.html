The National Heritage Museum. Since 1908.

Established in 1908, the National Heritage Museum is more than a museum—it is a living chronicle of human achievement, artistic brilliance, and scientific discovery. As the first institution of its kind in the region, we pioneered the preservation of cultural treasures and set the standard for museums worldwide.

Our halls have witnessed revolutions—both in art and thought. From the earliest archaeological finds to groundbreaking contemporary exhibitions, the National Heritage Museum has been a catalyst for curiosity, inspiring scholars, artists, and visitors for over two centuries.

Events & Workshops at The National Heritage Museum

At the National Heritage Museum, we believe that history should be experienced, not just observed. Our dynamic lineup of events, workshops, and interactive programs invites visitors of all ages to engage with culture, art, and science in unforgettable ways.
